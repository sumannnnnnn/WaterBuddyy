How We Calculate Your Water Needs

We use a personalized formula based on your body metrics and activity level:

  • Base calculation: 35ml per kg of body weight
  • Age adjustments: Slightly modified for younger and older individuals
  • Activity level: Based on your profession and typical daily activities
